Your Tradie Support

Your admin & marketing support partner

I help tradies and cleaning businesses with quotes, invoices, and basic marketing so you can spend less time on paperwork.

Services

1. Invoices & Quotes

We manage your quotes and invoices so you can focus on the job.

To begin: Provide your business name, logo, and bank account number.

Once set up: All we need from you is the customer details (name, address, email), job description, price, and payment method (message or text).

  • Creating professional quotes quickly
  • Sending invoices to clients promptly
  • Tracking payments and reminders

2. Basic Marketing

We help your business get noticed online with simple, effective marketing.

To begin: Share your social media accounts (or we can create them), business info, and branding materials.

Once set up: We post updates, monitor reviews, and ensure your online presence stays active and consistent.

  • Google Business Profile setup and updates
  • Facebook and Neighbourly posts to engage local customers
  • Builderscrack profile setup and monitoring
  • Simple, repeatable marketing tasks

How It Works

  1. Send us your tasks: Email your quotes, invoices, or marketing updates.
  2. We handle them: Prepare, format, and send quotes or invoices, and manage your online profiles.
  3. You focus on your work: Spend more time on the job site while we take care of admin and marketing.
  4. No contracts: Pay as you go, with full flexibility.

Everything is transparent — you can track what’s done and review results whenever you like.
