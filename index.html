Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What services do you offer?

We provide admin and business support for tradies, including invoicing & follow-ups, quotes, paperwork & filing, job bookings, and general office admin. Marketing support is offered separately if required.

What counts as an admin task?

An admin task is one clear, individual action such as creating and sending an invoice (including one standard follow-up), sending a quote, booking a job or appointment, filing or uploading paperwork for a job, or entering job details. Small updates or follow-ups that naturally form part of another task are included. Larger or more complex requests may be broken into multiple tasks or quoted separately.

Does sending an invoice include follow-up?

Yes. Creating and sending an invoice includes one standard follow-up if payment has not been received. Any additional follow-ups may count as extra admin tasks.

How long does it take to action a request?

Most standard admin tasks are completed within one business day. Urgent requests can be prioritised if needed — just let us know.

How do I send tasks to you?

Tasks can be sent via email, text, or Facebook Messenger — whatever works best for you. We’ll confirm once each task has been received and completed.

What if my request is bigger than a standard admin task?

If a task is more involved than a standard admin task, we’ll let you know before starting and confirm how it will be charged. Larger clean-ups, system setups, or ongoing projects are quoted separately.

Do you offer monthly support packages?

Yes. Monthly task bundles are perfect for tradies who want regular support. Packages are flexible and can be adjusted as your business needs change.

What happens if I don’t use all my monthly tasks?

Unused tasks can roll over once to the following month. This keeps things fair while giving you flexibility.

Do you offer urgent or same-day support?

Where possible, yes. Urgent or same-day requests depend on workload and availability.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No. There are no long-term contracts. Services are flexible to suit your business.

Who do you work with?

We work with tradies and small businesses, especially sole traders and small teams who want reliable admin support without hiring staff.

Why choose Your Tradie Support?

We’re a local Taranaki husband-and-wife team with experience in truck driving, logistics, accounts, and admin. We focus on making the paperwork side of business easy.

What do you need to get started?

All we need is one previous invoice or job sheet as an example. This usually includes company name, bank account details, customer details, and task description. From this, we can start taking work off your plate immediately.
