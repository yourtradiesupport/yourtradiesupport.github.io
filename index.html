Services

1. Invoices & Quotes

We handle your day-to-day quotes and invoicing so you can focus on the job. Our service includes:

  • Creating professional quotes quickly
  • Sending invoices to clients promptly
  • Tracking payments and reminders
  • Organizing records for GST and tax purposes

Getting started: Simply send us your business details, client list (optional), and any existing templates you use. We’ll set up a system that works for you.

After setup: We manage quotes, invoices, and reminders regularly. You just review or approve when needed.

2. Basic Marketing

We help your business get noticed online with simple, effective marketing. Tasks include:

  • Google Business Profile setup and updates
  • Facebook and Neighbourly posts to engage local customers
  • Builderscrack profile setup and monitoring
  • Simple, repeatable marketing tasks to keep your business visible

Getting started: Share your social media accounts (or we can create them), business information, and any branding materials you have.

After setup: We post updates, monitor reviews, and ensure your online presence stays active and consistent.

How It Works

Our process is designed to save you time and keep things simple:

  1. Send us your tasks: Email your quotes, invoices, or marketing updates.
  2. We handle them: We prepare, format, and send quotes or invoices, and manage your online profiles.
  3. You focus on your work: Spend more time on the job site while we take care of admin and marketing.
  4. No contracts: Pay as you go, with full flexibility.

Everything is transparent — you can track what’s done and review results whenever you like.
